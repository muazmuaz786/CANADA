About Canada

Canada is a large and peaceful country in North America. It is known for its beautiful nature, clean cities, and kind, respectful people.

🌍 Basic Information

  • Capital: Ottawa
  • Largest City: Toronto
  • Languages: English and French
  • Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD)
  • Famous Place: Niagara Falls
  • National Symbol: Maple Leaf
  • National Animal: Beaver

🎉 Canadian Culture

Canada is proud of its multicultural society. People from all around the world live in Canada, and all cultures are respected. Canadians believe in equality, kindness, and freedom.

What makes Canadian culture special?

  • You can find many different foods, music, and festivals from other countries in Canadian cities.
  • Canadians love the outdoors—hiking, camping, skiing, and ice skating are very popular.
  • Saying “sorry” and “thank you” is very common—Canadians are known for being very polite.
  • Hockey is not just a sport—it’s part of the national identity!

🍁 Canadian Holidays

  • Canada Day (July 1) – Canada’s birthday with parades and fireworks.
  • Thanksgiving (October) – Families come together to eat and give thanks.
  • National Indigenous Peoples Day (June 21) – Celebrating the First Nations, Inuit, and Métis peoples of Canada.

🍽️ Popular Canadian Foods

  • Poutine – French fries with cheese curds and gravy.
  • Maple syrup – A sweet syrup made from maple trees.
  • Butter tarts – A traditional Canadian dessert.
